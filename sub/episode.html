Genesis 2:1-3

The biblical Book of Genesis tells the story of God's creation, detailing the creation of heaven, earth, plants, animals, and the first humans. It also tells of the removal of Adam and Eve from the Garden of Eden as a consequence of the temptation from the 'tree of knowledge of good and evil.

Genesis 22:1-19

This story tells about the Promises of God to Isaac and how Abraham embraced the challenge that was given to him. By sacrificing his Son named Isaac, by that challenge Abraham showed his Faith to God and the willingness to obey God unconditionally without any doubts.
